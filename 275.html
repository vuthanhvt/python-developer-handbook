Appendix C. Python Copyright Notices

The following copyright notices provide the necessary credits to some of the material contained in this book.

Python's Copyright Notices supports Python itself, and most of the modules that are part of the standard distribution. Exceptions exist, such as the profile and the pstats modules, which have their own copyright notice. In Chapter 17, "Development Tools," we have part of the documentation and user manual of those modules covered by their copyright notice, which is presented in this appendix.

The last two copyrights listed in this section cover the JPython distribution. Depending on your choice of using or not the OROMatcher library (see Chapter 18, "JPython" ), you have a specific copyright notice to obey.
